Welcome to our crime prediction website! We are dedicated to helping individuals and communities stay safe by providing accurate and up-to-date information about crime trends and patterns in their area.

Our Mission

Crime prediction is the use of data and algorithms to try to predict where and when crimes are likely to occur. It is a complex and evolving field that involves analyzing data about past crimes and other relevant factors to identify patterns and trends that may indicate the likelihood of future crimes
